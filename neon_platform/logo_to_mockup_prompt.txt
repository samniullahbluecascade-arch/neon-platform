Task: High-End Indoor Lifestyle Product Render.

The Environment (The "User Delight" Factor):

Setting: A bright, modern, high-end indoor interior (e.g., a contemporary living room with a soft sofa, a boutique retail shop with clean shelves, or a minimalist office).

Lighting: The room is well-lit with natural daylight. No dark rooms. No pitch-black backgrounds.

Wall: The sign is mounted on a clean, textured white brick or light-oak wood paneled wall.

The Sign (The "Clean" Material):

No Visible Tubes: Do not render internal gas tubes or wires. Instead, render the design as Solid Glowing 3D Channels.

Graphic Sharpness: The edges of the glow must be crisp and clean, following the uploaded logo exactly.

Backer: A perfectly transparent "Ghost Acrylic" backer that follows the shape of the logo. It should have a subtle 3D thickness with a clean polished edge.

Composition & Scaling:

Center Hero: The sign must be the focal point, occupying 70-80% of the horizontal frame.

Straight-On View: Render from a direct, eye-level front perspective. No dramatic angles or warping.

Depth: Use a very shallow depth of field so the indoor background is slightly out of focus (bokeh), making the sharp, glowing sign "pop" off the screen.

Color & Mood:

Vibrant Glow: The glow should be intense and saturated (matching the user's color choice), but it must interact naturally with the bright daylight of the room.

Style: Modern, premium, "Pinterest-worthy" commercial photography.
